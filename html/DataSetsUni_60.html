Insurance claim R Documentation

The data represents the minimum insurance claim

Description

The function allows to provide the minimum insurance claim for every six month period from the 3rd of January 1980 to the 31 of December of 1990.

Usage

data_insurclaim

Arguments

data_insurclaim

A vector of (non-negative integer) values.

Details

The data represents the minimum insurance claim for every six month period from the 3rd of January 1980 to the 31 of December of 1990. Recently, it is used by Asgharzadeh et al. (2014) and fitted the Burr Poissonâ€“Lindley distribution.

Value

data_insurclaim gives the minimum insurance claim for every six month.
