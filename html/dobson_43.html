waist R Documentation

Waist loss data from table 2.8

Description

The weights, in kilograms, of twenty men before and after participation in a "waist loss" program

Usage

data(waist)

Format

A tibble with 20 observations and the following 3 variables.

man

man number

before

weight before in kgs

after

weight after in kgs

References

Egger, G., G. Fisher, S. Piers, K. Bedford, G. Morseau, S. Sabasio, B. Taipim, G. Bani, M. Assan, and P. Mills (1999). Abdominal obesity reduction in Indigenous men. International Journal of Obesity 23, 564â€“569.
