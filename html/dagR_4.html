addAngle R Documentation

Sum up two radian angles.

Description

Adds two radian angles together and applies modulus 2*pi. This is internally called by smoothArc, though hardly needed.

Usage

addAngle(a, b)

Arguments

a

Angle 1 in radian.

b

Angle 2 in radian.

Value

numeric value [0, 2*pi).
