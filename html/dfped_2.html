albAge R Documentation

Concentration of albumin according to age.

Description

Compute the value of albumin (alb) concentration (g/L) according to age (year) for children - Truncated at 10000 days, i.e. 27 y.o.

Usage

albAge(age)

Arguments

age

The age of child.
