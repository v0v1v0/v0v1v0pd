PlotGenAlg

Function for graphically representing the evolution.

Usage

PlotGenAlg(DGenes, dGenes, maxEval, meanEval)

Arguments

DGenes

Occurences of genes as dominant.
dGenes

Occurences of genes as recessive. For future developments.
maxEval

Maximum fitness.
meanEval

Average fitness.
