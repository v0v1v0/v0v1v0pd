has_step R Documentation

Does a ddPCR plate have a step with this name?

Description

Does a ddPCR plate have a step with this name?

Usage

has_step(plate, step)

Arguments

plate

A ddPCR plate
step

A step name

Examples

## Not run: 
dir <- sample_data_dir()
plate <- new_plate(dir)
steps(plate)
has_step(plate, 'REMOVE_FAILURES')
has_step(plate, 'NO_SUCH_STEP')

## End(Not run)
